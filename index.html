The Last Hike

A quick look at the fatalities that have occured at National Parks

THE IDEA

There's an alarming number of incidents that have occurred at the 423 national parks across the country that have lead to death. With over 230 millions visits in 2020, tourists may be unaware of the hidden risks of visiting one of these beautiful parks. Our intention is to spotlight those parks that have experienced numerous fatalities and should therefore, be avoided.
