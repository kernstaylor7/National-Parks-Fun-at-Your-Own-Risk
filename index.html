The Last Hike

A quick look at the fatalities that have occured at National Parks

THE IDEA

There's an alarming number of incidents that occur at the 423 national parks across the country that lead to death. With over 230 millions visits in 2020, tourists may be unaware of the risks of visiting these beautiful parks. Our intention is to spotlight those parks that have experienced over 50 incidents and should probably be avoided.
